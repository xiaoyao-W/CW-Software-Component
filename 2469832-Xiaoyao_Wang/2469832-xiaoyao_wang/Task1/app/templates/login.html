One platform for customers, operators, admins, and merchants.

Consolidated checkout, reliable order splitting/sync, real‑time stall fulfillment, and audited retries — all under role-based access.

Customer
  • Add items from multiple stalls into one cart and checkout once
  • Track one consolidated order with per‑stall fulfillment visibility
User (Ops)
  • Split a paid order into per‑stall sub‑orders and sync to each stall system
  • Auto‑generate a versioned flowchart of the order‑splitting logic on deploy/CI
Admin
  • Retry failed split/sync steps safely with full auditability
Merchant
  • View and update only my stall’s sub‑orders in real time
